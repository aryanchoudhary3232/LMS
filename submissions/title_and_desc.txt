Title: SeekhoBharat - Engagement-Driven E-Learning Platform

Description:
SeekhoBharat is an innovative e-learning platform designed to bridge the gap between structured academic environments like Google Classroom 
and open course marketplaces like Udemy. 
With a strong focus on student engagement and study productivity, 
SeekhoBharat offers a dynamic and interactive learning ecosystem.
The platform empowers educators to easily upload courses and foster active participation through built-in quizzes, 
interactive flashcards, and time-bound assignments with strict submission deadlines. 
To maintain a premium standard of education, SeekhoBharat implements a rigorous administrative validation process, 
ensuring that students are taught exclusively by verified and highly qualified teachers.
For students, the platform acts as both a learning hub and a productivity tool. 
Learners can monitor their daily study routines using an integrated study timer, 
visualize their progress on personalized dashboards, and build motivational learning streaks to stay on track. 
Furthermore, students can leave anonymous course reviews, fostering a transparent, feedback-driven community. 
By combining robust course management, gamified study tracking, and strict quality control, 
SeekhoBharat delivers a highly accountable and engaging online education experience.
